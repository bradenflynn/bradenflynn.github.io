01. Executive Summary

This project presents an institutional-grade analysis of NVIDIA Corporation, evaluating a multi-billion dollar headcount investment and stress-testing the current market valuation. The model answers two critical questions: Is the proposed hiring plan accretive to value? And can the current $190 share price be fundamentally justified?

Hiring Case

APPROVED. The model predicts a 37.0% steady-state ROIC, well above the 11.3% WACC hurdle rate.

Equity Case

CAUTION. DCF fair value is $108, suggesting the market is pricing in near-perfect execution & 32% growth.
